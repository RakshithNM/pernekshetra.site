Donate

We make a living by what we get. We make a life by what we give.

Dear all,
The Vaniya/Ganiga community is still growing and developing, and we see great potential and talent in our community that could reach greater heights. It's not just us; we realize that there are many people who share the same vision. We wish all those willing to contribute to come forward and work towards the welfare of the community. We are committed to developing the Kshethram with all your support.

Vidya Nidhi

We see that our kids need better education and a nice childhood. Most of our families are from a poor background and cannot support better education. The ‘VIDHYANIDHI’ initiative aims to make each and every kid of our community attend better schools and enjoy a lovely childhood. We also support higher education. An investment in knowledge pays the best interest. Donate!

Arogya Nidhi

There are situations in life when you need spontaneous assistance. We wish to have a healthy and fit Patali Ganiga community with fewer people affected by medical disorders. We have been sympathetic for long; now let's get empathetic, share the sorrow, and support people in our community who need medical assistance. Donate!

Temple Development

The sacred Perne Kshetra is well maintained and hosts a large number of devotees. The temple runs completely through the liberal and pious donations from the devotees. There is a lot of scope for the improvement of Kshetra and providing better facilities to the people. We appeal to you to make a generous contribution towards the development of the temple and give us strength.


Please write to pernekshetra@gmail.com to initiate your contribution.
